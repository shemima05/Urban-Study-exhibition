An Urban Odyssey

Situated on the sacred banks of the Godavari, Nashik is a tapestry of ancient rituals and modern growth. From the spiritual resonance of the Kumbh Mela to its emergence as India's wine capital, we've documented the city's pulse through the lens of urban architecture.
