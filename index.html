An Urban Study

Nashik is a historic city in the state of Maharashtra, situated along the banks of the Godavari River. Known for its deep religious significance, it is one of the key sites of the Kumbh Mela and attracts pilgrims from across the country. The city is also recognized for its growing urban development, pleasant climate, and prominence in India’s wine industry, often referred to as the “Wine Capital of India.”
